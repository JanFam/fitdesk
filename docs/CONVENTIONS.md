## Design Conventions

- **Colors**: Warm coral/orange primary (#EA580B), warm cream background (#FFEDD5), warm charcoal text (#1C1917). Never pure black text.
- **Typography**: Modern geometric or humanist sans-serif. Avoid cold system-ui defaults.
- **Visuals**: Real everyday people in photos. Warm natural lighting. Friendly coach energy — not gym bro, not corporate stock photo.
- **Layout**: Breathing room, rounded corners, soft shadows. Nothing cramped or aggressive.

## Things to Avoid

- Purple/dark themes (Everfit style)
- Cold corporate blue (#0077ff or similar)
- Stock fitness photos with gym lighting, abs, dumbbells
- Pure black (#000000) for text
- "Contact sales" pricing tiers for basic plans
- Nickel-and-dime add-on pricing (coach pain point we're solving)
